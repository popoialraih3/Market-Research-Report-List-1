<p><p>The smart water leak detectors market is primarily segmented into passive and active leak detectors. Passive leak detectors continuously monitor moisture levels and alert users only when a potential leak is detected, providing a cost-effective solution for early warning. In contrast, active leak detectors not only sense leaks but also take immediate action, such as shutting off water supplies or sending alerts to smartphones. This distinction reflects varying consumer needs for convenience, automation, and risk management in leak detection solutions.</p></p>
