<p><p>Dry lubricants are solid or semi-solid materials that offer lubrication without the need for liquid oils or greases. The advantages of dry lubricants include their ability to operate in extreme temperatures, resistance to water and chemical corrosion, and minimal environmental impact.</p><p>The Dry Lubricants Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period.
