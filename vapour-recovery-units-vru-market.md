<p><ul><li>Open-type Recovery</li><li>Closed-type Recovery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vapour Recovery Units (VRUs) are systems designed to capture and recover vapors emitted during the storage and transfer of liquid hydrocarbons. The market can be categorized into open-type recovery, which captures vapors and allows for some atmospheric exposure, and closed-type recovery, where vapors are contained and compressed without atmospheric interaction. Open-type systems are typically less costly but can result in higher emissions, while closed-type systems are more efficient in preventing emissions and are often used in more regulated environments.</p></p>
