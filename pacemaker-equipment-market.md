<p><strong>The Pacemaker Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Leadless Pacemaker</li><li>Single Chamber Pacemaker</li><li>Dual Chamber Pacemaker</li><li>Biventricular Pacemaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pacemaker equipment market consists of various types catering to different cardiac needs. Leadless pacemakers are wire-free devices implanted directly into the heart, reducing infection risks. Single chamber pacemakers are designed to regulate the heartbeat in one chamber, typically the right ventricle, while dual chamber pacemakers synchronize both the atrium and ventricle for improved heart function. Biventricular pacemakers are used in cardiac resynchronization therapy, treating heart failure by coordinating contractions in both ventricles for enhanced pumping efficiency.</p></p>
