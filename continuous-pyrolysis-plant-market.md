<p><p>The continuous pyrolysis plant market features two primary types: semi-continuous and fully continuous pyrolysis plants. Semi-continuous pyrolysis plants operate by processing feedstock in batches, allowing for periodic feeding and collection of outputs, offering flexibility and easier operation. In contrast, fully continuous pyrolysis plants enable a constant flow of materials, achieving uninterrupted processing and higher efficiency for larger-scale operations. Both types cater to varying industrial needs, focusing on waste-to-energy conversion and sustainable material management through thermal decomposition.</p></p>
