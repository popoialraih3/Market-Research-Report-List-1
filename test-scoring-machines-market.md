<p><p>The Test Scoring Machines market consists primarily of two types: Single Side Reading Type and Dual Side Reading Type. Single Side Reading Machines evaluate responses on one side of the answer sheet, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. In contrast, Dual Side Reading Machines scan both sides simultaneously, enhancing efficiency and accuracy by processing multiple data points at once. These technologies cater to different testing environments, influencing their adoption based on institutional needs and budget considerations.</p></p>
