<p><p>The bike rear lights market comprises two primary types: self-generating lights and battery-powered lights. Their eco-friendly nature appeals to cyclists who prioritize sustainability. In contrast, battery-powered lights offer convenience and portability, utilizing replaceable or rechargeable batteries for quick installation and easy maintenance.
