<p><ul><li>Transmission Electron Microscopes</li><li>Scanning Electron Microscopes</li><li>Dual-Beam (FIB/SEM) Systems</li><li>Spectroscopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nanomechanical testing systems encompass various market types including Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM), which provide high-resolution imaging of materials at the atomic level, and Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM), which visualize surface topography with great detail. Dual-Beam systems combine focused ion beam (FIB) technology with SEM for precise sample preparation and analysis. Spectroscopes analyze material properties through techniques like energy-dispersive X-ray spectroscopy (EDX), enhancing the understanding of nanomaterials' mechanical characteristics and applications in research and industry.</p></p>
