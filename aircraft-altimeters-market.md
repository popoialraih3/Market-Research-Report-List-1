<p><p>The aircraft altimeters market comprises two primary types: analog and digital altimeters. Analog altimeters utilize mechanical components and a dial display to indicate altitude, providing a traditional approach favored for its simplicity and reliability. In contrast, digital altimeters employ electronic sensors and provide altitude readings on digital displays, often integrating additional functionalities such as altitude alerts and data logging. The digital type is gaining traction due to advancements in technology, enhancing accuracy and usability in modern aviation.</p></p>
