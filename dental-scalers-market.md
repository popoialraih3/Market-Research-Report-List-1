<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive Dental Scalers</li><li>Piezoelectric Dental Scalers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental scalers market primarily consists of two types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric scalers. Magnetostrictive dental scalers utilize electromagnetic energy to produce rapid vibrations in tips, effectively removing plaque and calculus through a robust cleaning action. In contrast, piezoelectric dental scalers use ultrasonic vibrations generated by a crystal mechanism, offering precise and controlled scaling with less heat generation. Both types are integral to modern dentistry, enhancing oral hygiene and ensuring effective dental procedures while minimizing patient discomfort.</p></p>
