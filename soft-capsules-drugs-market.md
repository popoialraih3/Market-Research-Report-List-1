<p><p>The soft capsules drugs market is categorized primarily into two types: liquid core drugs and semi-solid core drugs. Liquid core drugs contain a free-flowing liquid formulation, allowing for rapid absorption and improved bioavailability. These are ideal for delivering oils, extracts, or other liquids effectively. In contrast, semi-solid core drugs feature a viscous gel or paste form, offering enhanced stability and a longer shelf life. Both types cater to diverse pharmaceutical needs, enhancing patient compliance through easier ingestion.</p></p>
