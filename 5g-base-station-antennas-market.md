<p><p>The 5G base station antennas market consists of passive and active antenna types. Passive antennas are traditional fixed antennas that rely on external processing units; they are cost-effective but lack advanced functionalities like beamforming. In contrast, active antennas integrate electronic components within the antenna unit itself, enabling enhanced capabilities such as dynamic beamforming and improved signal quality. This differentiation allows operators to choose based on performance requirements and budget, impacting deployment strategies and overall 5G network efficiency.</p></p>
