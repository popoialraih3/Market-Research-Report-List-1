<p><p>The TB disease vaccine market can be categorized into prevention vaccines, post-exposure vaccines, and others. Prevention vaccines aim to immunize individuals against tuberculosis before exposure, enhancing immunity in at-risk populations. Post-exposure vaccines target individuals who have been in contact with an infected person, bolstering their defenses against potential infection. "Others" may include therapeutic vaccines designed to aid those already infected or research-focused vaccines in development phases. Together, these categories address varied needs in TB management and prevention.</p></p>
