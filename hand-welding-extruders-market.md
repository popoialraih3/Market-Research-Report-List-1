<p><p>The hand welding extruders market primarily includes two types: contact welding and hot gas welding. Contact welding involves the direct application of heat and pressure to join materials, often using a heated nozzle to melt the surfaces being joined. Conversely, hot gas welding utilizes a focused stream of hot air or gas to melt the filler material and base materials, allowing for more flexibility in application. Both methods are essential for creating strong, reliable bonds in various industries, particularly plastics.</p></p>
