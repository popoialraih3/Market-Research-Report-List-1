<p><p>The Fiber Optic Ceramics market includes two main types: SC (Standard Connector) Fiber Optic Ceramics and LC (Lucent Connector) Fiber Optic Ceramics. SC Fiber Optic Ceramics feature a larger connector design, allowing for efficient coupling and higher performance in data transmission, typically used in telecom and data centers.
