<p><p>The containment building market comprises three primary types: Steel Containment, Concrete Containment, and Hybrid Containment. Steel Containment offers benefits like rapid construction and high strength-to-weight ratio, making it suitable for seismic applications. Concrete Containment, known for its durability and fire resistance, is often used for long-term facilities. Hybrid Containment combines both materials, leveraging the advantages of each to enhance structural integrity and cost efficiency. These types cater to specific project requirements in diverse industrial sectors, enhancing safety and resilience.</p></p>
