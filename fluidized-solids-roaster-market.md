<p><p>The Fluidized-Solids Roaster Market comprises two main types: the Straight Tube Furnace and the Upper Enlarged Furnace. The Straight Tube Furnace is designed for efficient thermal processing using a cylindrical configuration, promoting uniform heat distribution and optimal fluidization of solids. In contrast, the Upper Enlarged Furnace features a wider upper section, enhancing particle handling and increasing residence time, thus improving throughput. Both types are essential for various industrial applications, particularly in metallurgy and mineral processing, offering distinct advantages in operation and performance.</p></p>
