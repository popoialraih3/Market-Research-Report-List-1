<p><p>The chemical synthesizer market is primarily divided into two types: single mode and parallel mode. Single mode synthesizers focus on producing one compound at a time, optimizing for precision and control, making them ideal for complex reactions. In contrast, parallel mode synthesizers allow simultaneous synthesis of multiple compounds, enhancing throughput and efficiency. This dual approach caters to diverse research needs, enabling both detailed study and high-volume production, thus addressing the varying demands in chemical research and development.</p></p>
