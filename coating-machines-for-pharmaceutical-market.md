<p><p>Coating machines in the pharmaceutical market encompass various types designed for specific applications. Standard coating pans are traditional options that use gravity to coat tablets or pellets. Perforated coating pans utilize holes to enhance airflow and ensure uniform coating. Fluidized bed or air suspension coaters make use of controlled air streams to suspend particles, promoting uniform coating and drying. Additionally, other market options include advanced technologies like high-shear granulators and electrostatic coaters, catering to diverse pharmaceutical needs.</p></p>
