<p><ul><li>Mechanical Fusible Link</li><li>Electrical Fusible Link</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fusible link market encompasses two primary types: mechanical and electrical fusible links. Mechanical fusible links operate through temperature-sensitive materials that physically melt to trigger a mechanical action, commonly used in fire protection systems and HVAC applications. In contrast, electrical fusible links function by using conductive materials that break a circuit when exposed to excessive heat, often found in electronic devices and automotive applications. Both types are essential for safety mechanisms, ensuring efficient operation and protection from overheating and fires.</p></p>
