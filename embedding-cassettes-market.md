<p><p>The embedding cassettes market is primarily divided into two types: glass embedding cassettes and polymethylpentene embedding cassettes. Glass cassettes are favored for their durability, chemical resistance, and ability to withstand high temperatures during processing, making them ideal for histology applications. In contrast, polymethylpentene cassettes are lightweight, transparent, and offer superior transparency for easier specimen visualization. Both types cater to laboratory needs, with the choice depending on specific application requirements, such as temperature tolerance and specimen handling preferences.</p></p>
