<p><p>The industrial gearbox market consists of various types designed for specific applications. Helical gearboxes feature angled teeth for smooth operation and high torque capabilities, making them ideal for heavy machinery. Worm reduction gearboxes offer high torque in a compact design with a unique screw-and-nut mechanism, suitable for low-speed applications. Planetary gearboxes provide high efficiency and compactness, using multiple gears for performance optimization. Other types include bevel gearboxes and cycloidal gearboxes, catering to diverse industrial needs and preferences.</p></p>
