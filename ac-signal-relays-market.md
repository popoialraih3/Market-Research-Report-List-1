<p><p>The AC signal relays market comprises various mounting types, primarily TH (Through-Hole) and SMT (Surface-Mount Technology). TH mounting involves inserting relay leads into pre-drilled holes on a printed circuit board, offering robust connections favored in high-power applications. Conversely, SMT mounting soldering components directly onto the PCB surface allows for compact designs and improved assembly efficiency. Both types cater to different application needs, impacting overall performance, reliability, and manufacturing processes across industries such as automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
