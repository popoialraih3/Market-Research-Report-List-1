<p><p>The Smart PV Array Combiner Box market is segmented into two main types: DC Smart PV Combiner Boxes and AC Smart PV Combiner Boxes. DC Smart PV Combiner Boxes aggregate the output of multiple photovoltaic (PV) strings and enhance system efficiency by providing overcurrent protection and monitoring capabilities.
