<p><p>The Aerospace & Defense ducting market comprises three primary types: rigid, semi-rigid, and flexible ducting. Rigid ducting is sturdy and designed for fixed installations, providing durability and structural integrity. Semi-rigid ducting combines flexibility with rigidity, allowing it to adapt to slightly curved paths while maintaining a stable shape. Flexible ducting, on the other hand, offers maximum adaptability, enabling easy routing around obstacles and tight spaces. Each type caters to specific operational requirements, ensuring efficient airflow and temperature control in aerospace and defense applications.</p></p>
