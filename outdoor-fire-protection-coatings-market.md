<p><p>Outdoor fire protection coatings are crucial for enhancing the fire resistance of structures exposed to external elements. The market is divided into two main types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based coatings are eco-friendly, easy to apply, and provide efficient fire resistance with low volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, solvent-based coatings offer superior durability and adhesion, making them suitable for harsh environments. Both types cater to various applications, balancing fire safety with environmental considerations.</p></p>
