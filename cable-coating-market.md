<p><strong>The Cable Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IntumescentCableCoatings</li><li>Non-IntumescentCableCoatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cable coating market primarily consists of two types: intumescent and non-intumescent cable coatings. Intumescent cable coatings expand when exposed to heat, creating a protective char layer that insulates cables from fire damage and prolongs their integrity during a fire event. In contrast, non-intumescent cable coatings provide a barrier against moisture, chemicals, and abrasion but do not expand under heat. Both types are essential for enhancing fire safety and extending the lifespan of electrical installations in various environments.</p></p>
