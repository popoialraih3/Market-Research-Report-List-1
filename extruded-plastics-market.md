<p><p>The extruded plastics market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is valued for its flexibility and resilience in packaging. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is known for its strength and durability, commonly used in containers and piping. Polypropylene features excellent chemical resistance, making it ideal for automotive and medical applications. Polystyrene is lightweight and cost-effective, often found in packaging and insulation. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) offers versatility in construction and plumbing due to its rigidity and weather resistance.</p></p>
