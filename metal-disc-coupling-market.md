<p><strong>The Metal Disc Coupling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Disc Couplings</li><li>Double Disc Couplings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal disc coupling market is primarily segmented into single disc couplings and double disc couplings. Single disc couplings consist of a single flexible disc, providing simple, low-cost solutions effective for torque transmission and accommodating angular misalignment. In contrast, double disc couplings incorporate two flexible discs separated by a spacer, allowing greater flexibility and the ability to handle higher backlash and misalignment. Both types are widely utilized in industrial applications for their durability and efficient power transfer capabilities.</p></p>
