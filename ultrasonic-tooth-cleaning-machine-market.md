<p><p>The ultrasonic tooth cleaning machine market comprises two main types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric ultrasonic dental cleaner machines. Magnetostrictive machines utilize a magnetic field to vibrate cleaning tips, effectively removing plaque and calculus from teeth. In contrast, piezoelectric machines employ crystal oscillation to create ultrasonic vibrations, offering precise and efficient cleaning. Both types enhance dental hygiene through minimally invasive procedures, catering to various patient needs and preferences, thus driving their popularity in modern dental practices.</p></p>
