<p><p>The 3D Machine Vision Camera market comprises several technologies: </p><p>1. **Stereo Vision Cameras** use two or more lenses to capture images from different angles, enabling depth perception through triangulation.</p><p>2. **Structured Light Cameras** project a series of light patterns onto objects to capture 3D shapes and surfaces.</p><p>3. **Time-of-Flight (ToF) Cameras** measure the time light takes to bounce back from surfaces, generating precise distance data.</p><p>4. **Laser Triangulation Cameras** utilize laser beams and calculate distances based on the angle of reflection. </p><p>5. **Others** include various emerging technologies and hybrid systems, enhancing 3D vision capabilities across industries.</p></p>
