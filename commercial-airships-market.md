<p><strong>The Commercial Airships Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid</li><li>Semi Rigid</li><li>Non-rigid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial airships market is categorized into three types: rigid, semi-rigid, and non-rigid. Rigid airships maintain their shape with a solid framework, allowing for larger internal volumes and better performance. Semi-rigid airships combine a structural frame with a gas envelope, offering flexibility and moderate payload capacity. Non-rigid airships, or blimps, rely solely on internal gas pressure for shape, making them lightweight and cost-effective. Each type serves distinct applications, from tourism to advertising and surveillance, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
