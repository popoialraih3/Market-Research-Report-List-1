<p><strong>The Flu Vaccine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trivalent Influenza Vaccine</li><li>Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flu vaccine market is primarily categorized into two types: trivalent and quadrivalent influenza vaccines. Trivalent vaccines target three flu strains, typically two A strains and one B strain, providing essential protection during flu seasons. In contrast, quadrivalent vaccines offer broader coverage by including an additional B strain, enabling better defense against circulating viruses. The choice between them depends on various factors, including population needs and specific recommendations, with quadrivalent vaccines generally preferred for their enhanced protection.</p></p>
