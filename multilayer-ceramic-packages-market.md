<p><strong>The Multilayer Ceramic Packages Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ceramic–Metal Sealing (CERTM)</li><li>Glass–Metal Sealing (GTMS)</li><li>Passivation Glass</li><li>Transponder Glass</li><li>Reed Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multilayer Ceramic Packages encompass various types distinguished by their sealing and material properties. Ceramic–Metal Sealing (CERTM) integrates ceramics with metals, ensuring durability and thermal stability. Glass–Metal Sealing (GTMS) provides a hermetic seal, often utilized in sensitive electronic components. Passivation Glass protects circuit elements, enhancing reliability. Transponder Glass is designed for wireless communication applications, enabling efficient signal transmission. Lastly, Reed Glass is used in magnetic sensors, providing effective switching capabilities. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to diverse electronic applications.</p></p>
