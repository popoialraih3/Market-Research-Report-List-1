<p><ul><li>Low-To-Mid Field</li><li>HighField</li><li>Very HighField</li><li>Ultra-High Field</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Closed MRI systems are categorized by their magnetic field strength, influencing image quality and diagnostic capabilities. Low-to-mid field MRI systems, typically ranging from 0.2 to 1.5 Tesla, are more affordable and less claustrophobic but offer lower resolution. High-field systems, around 1.5 to 3 Tesla, provide better image clarity and faster scanning times. Very high-field systems (3 to 7 Tesla) enhance resolution for detailed studies, while ultra-high field MRI systems (above 7 Tesla) are primarily used for advanced research applications, delivering unparalleled imaging precision.</p></p>
