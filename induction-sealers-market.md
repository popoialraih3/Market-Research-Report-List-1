<p><p>Induction sealers are sealing devices that utilize electromagnetic induction to create a hermetic seal on containers, typically made of plastic or glass. These sealers work by applying heat to a foil liner placed inside the cap or lid, causing the liner to melt and bond with the container's rim. This process not only enhances product freshness and shelf life but also provides tamper evidence, making it a preferred choice across various industries, including food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.</p><p>The Induction Sealers Market is witnessing robust growth, driven by increased demand for secure packaging solutions and rising consumer awareness about product safety.
