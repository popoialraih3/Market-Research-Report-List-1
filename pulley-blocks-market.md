<p><p>The pulley blocks market is primarily categorized into two types: fixed pulleys and moving pulleys. Fixed pulleys are stationary devices that change the direction of force applied, allowing users to lift loads more easily without mechanical advantage. In contrast, moving pulleys, which attach to the load, provide mechanical advantage by reducing the effort needed to lift heavier objects. Both types are essential in various applications, including construction, shipping, and automotive industries, contributing to efficient load management and transportation.</p></p>
