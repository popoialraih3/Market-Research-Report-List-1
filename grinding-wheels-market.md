<p><p>The grinding wheels market primarily consists of resinoid bond and vitrified bond types. Resinoid bond grinding wheels employ synthetic resin for strong adhesion, offering flexibility and effective heat dissipation, making them suitable for a variety of materials. Vitrified bond grinding wheels, made from ceramic materials, provide exceptional rigidity and durability, ideal for precision applications. Both types cater to specific machining needs, influencing their adoption across industries such as metalworking, automotive, and aerospace for efficient surface finishing and material removal.</p></p>
