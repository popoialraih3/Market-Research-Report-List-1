<p><p>Digital compasses are categorized by the number of sensors they utilize to detect orientation and motion. </p><p>- **3-axis type**: These compasses measure magnetic fields along three axes, providing basic directional information.</p><p>  </p><p>- **6-axis type**: This variant includes accelerometers, integrating motion detection for enhanced accuracy and tilt compensation.</p><p>  </p><p>- **9-axis type**: This type combines 3-axis magnetometers, 3-axis accelerometers, and 3-axis gyroscopes, offering comprehensive orientation and motion tracking, making it ideal for advanced applications in robotics and navigation systems.</p></p>
