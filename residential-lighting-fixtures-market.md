<p><p>The residential lighting fixtures market includes several key types. Incandescent bulbs offer warm light but are energy-inefficient. Fluorescent lighting, while more efficient and longer-lasting, can be harsh in tone and may contain harmful substances. LED fixtures are energy-efficient, have a long lifespan, and provide versatile color options, making them popular. OLED technology features organic compounds that emit light, allowing for thin, flexible designs with even illumination, though they tend to be pricier and less common in standard residential use.</p></p>
