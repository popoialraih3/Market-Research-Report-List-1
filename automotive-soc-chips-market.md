<p><p>The Automotive SoC (System-on-Chip) market features several types that cater to diverse vehicle needs. CPUs (Central Processing Units) manage general tasks and system operations, while GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) handle complex graphics and visualization. DSPs (Digital Signal Processors) are tailored for audio and video processing. ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuits) are custom-designed for specific functions, enhancing efficiency. FPGAs (Field-Programmable Gate Arrays) offer flexibility for reconfigurable applications. Other categories may include integrated sensor chips or communication processors, contributing to advanced automotive functionalities.</p></p>
