<p><p>Ambulifts are specialized vehicles designed for patient transportation, particularly in airports. The market can be divided into two main types: SideBull and FrontBull. SideBull ambulifts feature a side-loading mechanism, allowing for efficient entry and exit from the vehicle, which is particularly useful in tight spaces. In contrast, FrontBull ambulifts are designed with a front-loading system, providing easier access and visibility when maneuvering patients. Both types enhance accessibility and streamline patient transfer processes in various environments.</p></p>
