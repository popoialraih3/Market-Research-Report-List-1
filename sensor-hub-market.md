<p><p>The sensor hub market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Application Sensor Processor**: Dedicated processors designed for specific applications, providing advanced data processing and low power consumption for sensors in various applications. </p><p>2. **Discrete Sensor Processor**: Standalone processors that manage different sensors separately, enabling flexibility in sensor integration and performance optimization. </p><p>3. **Sensor Integrated Microcontroller**: Combines sensor processing capabilities directly within a microcontroller, streamlining design and reducing costs while enhancing communication between sensors and processing units.</p></p>
