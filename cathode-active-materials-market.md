<p><ul><li>NCA</li><li>NMC</li><li>LFP</li><li>LMO</li><li>LCO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cathode active materials market encompasses various types utilized in battery technologies. NCA (Nickel Cobalt Aluminum) is known for high energy density, making it suitable for electric vehicles. NMC (Nickel Manganese Cobalt) balances energy density, cost, and stability. LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) offers safety and longevity, ideal for power tools and electric buses. LMO (Lithium Manganese Oxide) provides thermal stability and is often used in power applications. LCO (Lithium Cobalt Oxide) is widely used in consumer electronics due to its high energy density.</p></p>
