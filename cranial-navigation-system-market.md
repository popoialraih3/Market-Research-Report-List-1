<p><strong>The Cranial Navigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical System</li><li>Electromagnetic System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cranial Navigation System market is primarily divided into two types: Optical Systems and Electromagnetic Systems. Optical Systems utilize camera-based technology to capture spatial data and guide surgical instruments through visual tracking. They offer high precision but can be limited by line-of-sight issues. In contrast, Electromagnetic Systems employ magnetic fields to determine the position of instruments relative to the patient’s anatomy, providing more flexibility in movement. Both systems enhance surgical accuracy and safety in cranial procedures.</p></p>
